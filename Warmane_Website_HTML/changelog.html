CLASSES

  • Melee hit rating has been corrected and is now capped at 8% report #188
  • Fixed the issue where abilities such as Misdirection, Stealth, Shadowform, Summon Ghouls would sometimes have an infinite cooldown report #1406 and report #314
  • Movement impairing abilities such as Judgement of Justice, Focused Assault or Brutal Assault to be removed by snare removing spells such as Hand of Freedom, Master's Call or using a pvp trinket corrected report #401
  • Mechanics that grant an extra attack on abilities (such as Hack and Slash, Sword Specialization etc) have been reworked report #98
  • Fixed an issue where abilities such as Seal of Corruption and Vengeance would be affected twice by damage modifiers such as Avenging Wrath report #211

DEATH KNIGHT

  • Death Knight rune cooldown reduction system has been fixed. Report #153
  • Death and Decay's threat will now be increased by Frost Presence. report #321
  • Rune Refreshing spells like Glyph of Scourge Strike will now always pick the rune with the longest remaining cooldown. Report #177
  • Glyph of Scourge Strike will now affect the duration of Ebon Plaguebringer as well
  • Icy Clutch will now also be dispelled when Frost Fever is dispelled
  • Raise Dead will now again require Corpse Dust if there's no corpse nearby

HUNTER

  • Fixed trap selection to always pick the closest target. report #2015
  • Explosive Trap DoT will now properly scale with RAP
  • Master's Call will no longer be useable on confused, feared or stunned pets
  • Snake Trap snakes will no longer attack Crowd Controlled units

MAGE

  • Torment the Weak will now properly affect bosses. Report #301
  • Focus Magic should now properly stack with 5% crit auras. It should also no longer stack with itself report #323
  • Fixed buffs and debuffs sometimes vanishing for mages Report #208
  • Weapon auras are now added to the target instead of the caster, preventing buff overflow. Report #208
  • Mirror Image causing big threat spike if Hand of Salvation is cast with it fixed
  • Pyroblast will now remove Hot Streak on cast< / li>

PRIEST

  • Mind Flay proccing Misery and other negative effects on the caster itself fixed
  • Mind Control now be interrupted by Spirit of Redemption
  • Renewed Hope will now properly affect Flash Heal

ROGUE

  • Envenom will no longer be dispelled by positive poison - cleansing spells

SHAMAN

  • Glyph of Feral Spirit will now correctly give 40% of your AP to your report #605
  • Windfury Weapon will now be handled properly for offhand procs
  • Hex, Chain Heal, Lesser Healing Wave, Healing Wave, Lightning Bolt, Chain Lightning will now remove Maelstrom Weapon on cast

WARLOCK

  • Shadow Bolt will now remove Shadow Trance on cast

WARRIOR

  • Defensive Stance will now correctly apply threat modifiers. Report #332
  • Charge and similar effects now avoid fall damage. Report #10
  • Charge will now move to max melee range, not object model size
  • Fixed threat generation of Shield Slam and Concussion Blow. Report #2160
  • Unrelenting Assault will no longer sometimes proc if the target has just casted an instant spell

BATTLEGROUNDS AND ARENAS
